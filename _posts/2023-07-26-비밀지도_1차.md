 1.  zfill(width) 함수 사용

```python
"2".zfill(3)`
"002"`

`"50000".zfill(5)`

"123".zfill(5)`
"00123"`
```


## 3. 다른 사람 풀이

![o3](https://github.com/mini0-0/mini0-0.github.io/assets/63296983/bcfb4bd9-a731-470f-9369-88d4f6858f92)

1. **rjust(width, [fillchar]) 함수** 사용

```python
"2".rjust(3, "0")
#"002"`

"50000".rjust(5, "0")
#"50000"

"123".rjust(5, "0")
#"00123"

"123".rjust(5, "a")
#"aa123"
```
